Activate the NFSFU234FormValidation Extension
  1. Click on the extension icon.
  2. Look for NFSFU234FormValidation Extension
  3. Click on the pin icon beside it (optional)
  4. Click on the NFSFU234FormValidation Extension
  5. A popup will show and you will see the "Enable Form Validation" button
  6. Look on your left screen and close this pop over
  7. Then try submitting the form again. without any input and it should show you some errors
